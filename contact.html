How We Work with RIAs

Our strategies are made available under a model licensing agreement. Each quarter, we deliver a rebalancing spreadsheet that RIAs implement directly within their SMA infrastructure — using tools like Orion or comparable platforms.

This is a non-discretionary model: the RIA retains full control over implementation and maintains the client relationship. We provide the research, the methodology, and the quarterly guidance.

The Two Strategies

BBY Consistent Buyback Yield

Screens for companies with consistent buyback yield over a 5-year window — a total yield analog to dividend achievers. ~125 holdings.

BBY High Total Yield

Selects S&P 500 companies by highest combined dividend and buyback yield, optimized for current income. ~110 holdings.

Both strategies have outperformed the S&P 500 over 20-year backtested periods.

Equity model portfolios built on total shareholder yield — for RIAs seeking durable, differentiated income strategies.
