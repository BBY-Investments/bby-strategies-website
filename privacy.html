Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

BBY Strategies ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at bbystrategies.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you visit our Site or communicate with us.

We take your privacy seriously. This Site is informational in nature and we do not collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide when contacting us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly. When you contact us, you may provide your name, email address, firm name, and a message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Automatically collected information. Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log information, including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used for security and operational purposes only.
  • Cookies. This Site uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you about our strategies and services.
  • To send follow-up communications if you have expressed interest in our model portfolios (you may opt out at any time).
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email Communications

If you contact us or subscribe to communications from BBY Strategies, you may receive emails from us related to our strategies, research, and services. Each email will include an unsubscribe link. You may opt out of email communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly at partnerships@bbystrategies.com.

We take email deliverability and anti-spam practices seriously. We will only send communications to individuals who have expressed interest in our services or who we have a legitimate business reason to contact.

Third-Party Services

This Site uses Google Fonts, loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect limited information as part of serving font files. We do not use Google Analytics or other tracking tools on this Site.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a business relationship. If you ask us to delete your information, we will do so to the extent required by applicable law.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information you provide to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of any information you transmit to us.

Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for business and investment professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise any such rights, please contact us at partnerships@bbystrategies.com. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
